This repository is the implementation of a rendezvous hash mechanism that is data center and availability zone aware. 

When people learn about Elixir and Erlang, there are a few truly inspiring videos that demonstrate the nature of these technologies, and the power of the BEAM platform. One of these videos is [The Soul of Erlang and Elixir, by Saša Jurić](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JvBT4XBdoUE). For me, being interested in resiliency and high availability of distributed systems, the other one is a series of videos by Bryan Hunter about Project Waterpark: Transforming Healthcare with Distributed Actors (videos [1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9qUfX3XFi_4) (4 years old), [2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pQ0CvjAJXz4) (2 years old) and [3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hdBm4K-vvt0) (the most recent one)).
